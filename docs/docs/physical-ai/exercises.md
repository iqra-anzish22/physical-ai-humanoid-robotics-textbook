# Physical AI Exercises

## Exercise 1: Embodied Cognition Analysis

<Exercise
  title="Embodied Cognition in Robotic Systems"
  difficulty="intermediate"
  type="theoretical"
  instructions="Analyze a robotic system of your choice and identify how its physical form influences its cognitive capabilities. Consider the relationship between the robot's sensors, actuators, and its ability to understand and interact with its environment."
  expectedOutcome="Students will understand how embodiment shapes cognitive processes in robotic systems and be able to analyze the relationship between physical form and intelligence."
/>

## Exercise 2: Simulation vs. Reality Comparison

<Exercise
  title="Addressing the Reality Gap"
  difficulty="advanced"
  type="practical"
  instructions="Research a specific example where a robot trained in simulation performed differently in the real world. Analyze the factors that contributed to the performance gap and propose strategies to mitigate the reality gap in future implementations."
  expectedOutcome="Students will understand the challenges of transferring skills from simulation to reality and be able to propose solutions to address the reality gap problem."
/>
